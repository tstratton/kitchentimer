---- 1.0.8 - Jul 18, 2010

+ Added more popups with instructions on how to turn off the alarm O_o
@ It seems that lately many people are not able to read and/or understand the notice displayed at startup explaining how to turn offthe alarm:
you should TOUCH THE RED TIMER OR USE/OPEN THE NOTIFICATION BAR.
Some people have removed the battery to turn off the timer and voted one star on the Market.
I am very disappointed, because I have always shown instructions at startup on how to turn it off in order to avoid these very problems.
Now you see the same notification also when a timer starts and when a countdown ends.
I hope this will avoid the unnecessary removal of other batteries.
This application is free, without any ads and is open source. If you like it please write a comment on the Android Market.
